Description:

Welcome to the Word Scramble Game! In this game, you'll test your
word-solving skills by unscrambling letters to form words.
Race against the clock to maximize your score and compete with your friends.
Can you unscramble them all?

Rules:

  1. You have 30 seconds to unscramble as many words as possible.
  2. A scrambled word will be presented on the screen.
  3. Rearrange the letters to form the correct word as quickly as you can.
  4. Your score is based on your speed
  5. Speed Bonus: The faster you solve a word, the more points you earn.
  6. Be careful! Incorrect answers result in a time penalty.
